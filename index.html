Who we are

Queenstown's
full-time portrait team.

We are a team of professional portrait photographers based in Queenstown, New Zealand. Our passion is guiding people on a journey of love and adventure, crafting comfortable, fun sessions that capture the essence of who you are together.

Unlike freelance visitors, we are here year-round. We know the light, the seasons, and the hidden corners of every location. That local knowledge shows in every frame we deliver.

We work under Nomado Ltd — the same team behind New Zealand's leading elopement and heli-wedding service. You get that same standard of care, attention, and photography for your vacation.
